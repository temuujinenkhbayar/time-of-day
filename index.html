Time of Day by Temuujin Enkhbayar

What time is it?

This page will change based on the hour of the day.

This page uses JavaScript to determine the current hour, and presents a different image, custom message, and color based on each of the following times of day:

  • Morning (5am to 12 noon)
  • Afternoon (12 noon to 6pm)
  • Evening (6pm to 11pm)
  • Night (11pm to 5am)

To do this, I'm using a compound if block and selecting ranges and using the getHours() method of the Date() object.

To make changes, I use document.getElementById and document.querySelector to identify items on the page to change.
